Wildflowers Travelling Tattoo is Nansi Twice. The focus of the project is helping others; helping them have meaningful, uplifting, or just really fucking cool tattoos. Nansi works in microrealistic or typographical styles. Her work is a little bit spiritual, a little bit healing, and all empowering.

Wildflowers plan on doing guest spots around the country, as well as festivals, events, and weddings.

Along with tattoos, Wildflowers will also be selling handmade jewellery, tote bags, tea towels, and art to begin with. All vegan and as environmentally friendly as possible. Eventually Wildflowers will offer wellbeing sessions and art classes.
