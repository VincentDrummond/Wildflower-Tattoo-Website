About Wildflowers

Wildflowers Travelling Tattoo is Nansi Twice. The focus of the project is helping others; helping them have meaningful, uplifting, or just really fucking cool tattoos.

Nansi works in microrealistic or typographical styles. Her work is a little bit spiritual, a little bit healing, and all empowering.

Along with tattoos, Wildflowers will also be selling handmade jewellery, tote bags, tea towels, and art to begin with. All vegan and as environmentally friendly as possible.

Nansi also plans on doing tattoo guest spots around the country, as well as festivals, events, and weddings. Eventually she will offer wellbeing sessions and art classes.
